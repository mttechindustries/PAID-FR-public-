About the Project

PAID-FR is a pioneering research initiative focused on developing next-generation field repulsion systems. By integrating advanced plasma physics with artificial intelligence, we aim to unlock new possibilities in propulsion, shielding, and energy manipulation.

  • Goals: Advance the science of field repulsion for peaceful, sustainable applications in aerospace, energy, and defense.
  • Applications: Space propulsion, radiation shielding, energy transfer, and advanced materials.
  • Societal Impact: Enable safer space travel, clean energy solutions, and new frontiers in scientific discovery.

How It Works

PAID-FR leverages artificial intelligence to design and optimize plasma-based field repulsion systems. By simulating millions of configurations, our AI identifies the most effective approaches for manipulating electromagnetic fields. These designs are validated through a combination of laboratory experiments and advanced simulations, ensuring both scientific rigor and real-world applicability.

  • AI-driven optimization of plasma and metamaterial structures
  • Iterative simulation and experimental validation
  • Open data and transparent research practices
